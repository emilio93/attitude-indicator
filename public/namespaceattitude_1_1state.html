◆ ADC_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR

const uint16_t attitude::state::ADC_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR = ADC_Z_MAX_VARIATION >> 6

The factor by which the measurement needs to be divided in order to map to the lcd screen.

Formula is ADX_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR = 2*ADX_Z_MAX_VARIATTION/ScreenResolution

For a 128 resolution screen ADX_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR = 2/128*ADX_Z_MAX_VARIATTION ADX_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR = 1/64*ADX_Z_MAX_VARIATTION ADX_Z_SCREEN_FACTOR = ADX_Z_MAX_VARIATTION>>6;
